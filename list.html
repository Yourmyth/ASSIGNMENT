DESCRIPTIVE LIST OF STEPS IN COOKING RICE AND STEW

Step 1
Boil the Meat and Fish with seasoning for 10 minutes
Step 2
Fry or grill the cooked fish or meat until golden brown
Step 3
-Blend the pepper, tomatoes and onions until smooth
-Boil to reduce excess water
Step 4
Fry the stew base: Heat oil and pour in the blended pepper mix until the oil seperates
Step 5
-Add the fried meat and fish stock and the seasoning
-Mix for few minutes and remove from fire
Step 6
Wash the rice thorougly: Boil the rice with salted water, until soft
Remove excess water
Step 7
Serve hot rice with flavoured stew and meat
